Core Characteristics of Gemini
Symbol: The Twins (representing duality, adaptability, and versatility).
Element: Air (intellectual, communicative, and social).
Modality: Mutable (flexible, adaptable, and restless).
Ruling Planet: Mercury (governs communication, intellect, and curiosity).
Key Traits: Highly sociable ("social butterflies"), curious, witty, charming, and analytical.
Weaknesses: Can be indecisive, prone to boredom, inconsistent, or gossip-prone. 

Personality Breakdown
The Communicator: Gemini is excellent at initiating conversations, networking, and picking up new knowledge quickly.
Social Butterfly: They thrive in social settings, buzzing between different groups, and are often seen as playful, charming, and entertaining.
Adaptable & Curious: They are fast learners who hate routine and boredom, preferring to explore new ideas, places, or hobbies constantly.
Dual Nature: Due to their "twins" symbol, they can quickly switch between personalities or viewpoints, making them great at seeing all sides of a situation, but sometimes appearing inconsistent or "two-faced". 

Common Gemini Behaviors
